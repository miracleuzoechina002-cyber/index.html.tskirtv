About the Show

TSKIR (pronounced skrrrt) is a satirical reality‑TV style series where iconic women of the Bible tell their side: the love, the politics, the mess, and the miracles. Confessionals, dramatic reenactments, and reunion‑episode energy—served with contemporary fashion and unapologetic humor.

  • ✦ Confessional chair interviews powered by AI avatars.
  • ✦ Over‑the‑top ensemble drama inspired by scripture.
  • ✦ Bite‑size episodes perfect for socials and streaming.

Queen energy. Ocean‑slick waves. Gold cuffs. Her confessional reads are gentle but lethal.

Velvet hood. Blade‑sharp eyeliner. She speaks in soft tones and ends scenes in silence.
